About

Public proof layer for an AI-assisted decision system — not investment advice.

What this is

Singularity Ledger is a verified decision record. Every buy, sell, reject, exit, catalyst call, and probability forecast the system publishes gets a timestamped receipt. Later, market outcomes grade those claims mechanically. Misses stay visible on purpose.

What this is not

  • Not a signal service — no ticker recommendations are sold here.
  • Not a fund marketing page — position sizes and account values are scrubbed before publish.
  • Not retroactive marketing — records marked ARCHIVE were backfilled from an internal warehouse; LIVE tier means the claim entered the chain while still open.
