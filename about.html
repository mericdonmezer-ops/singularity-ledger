About

Decision infrastructure for teams building agents that must be accountable after deployment.

What this is

Decision Ledger turns consequential agent decisions into verifiable receipts. Each receipt records what the agent decided, what authority it had, what evidence it cited, and when the result should be checked. The outcome is graded later, so teams can measure whether an agent is actually improving instead of trusting a demo.

This public site is the Singularity reference implementation: a real, noisy decision stream used to prove the engine in public. The product is the generic receipt, grading, integrity, and governance layer that another team can run against its own agent workflow.

What this is not

  • Not a signal service — the product does not tell you which stock to buy or which model to deploy.
  • Not a chat transcript archive — structured claims, authority, and resolution windows are first-class data.
  • Not retroactive marketing — LIVE means the receipt entered the chain while its outcome was still unknown; ARCHIVE is disclosed historical backfill.
