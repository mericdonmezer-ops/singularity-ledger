How to read this
Awaiting outcome = armed, not proven.

These rows are published and waiting for the market window to close.

GRADED = the market answered.

Resolved rows feed Brier score, rejection quality and sell-side churn metrics.

LIVE = the real forward test.

0 records (0%) were first published before the outcome was knowable; ARCHIVE rows are honest bootstrap history.

Two tiers, honestly labeled. LIVE records entered the chain before their outcome was knowable — that is the verifiable claim. ARCHIVE records are backfilled internal history published retroactively: real, unedited, but they cannot claim pre-registration. We label them instead of pretending.
